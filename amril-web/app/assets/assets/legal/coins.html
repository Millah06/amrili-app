01What coins are

Coins are a digital item for use inside Amril. When you obtain coins, you receive a limited licence to use them within the app for the features described below. Coins are not money, not a deposit, and not legal tender. Coins you buy have no monetary value and are not redeemable for cash.

02How you get coins

  • Buying coins — inside Nigeria, you can buy coins using the app’s payment methods (through our licensed payment partners). On iOS and Android, coins may be bought through the Apple App Store or Google Play, in which case Apple or Google process the purchase under their terms.
  • Receiving coins — other users can gift you coins.
  • Earning coins — you may receive coins through eligible activity in the app, as described where the feature is offered.

03What you can do with coins

  • Gift coins to posts and to other users to show support.
  • Promote or boost your content so more people see it.

Using coins this way consumes them — gifting and promoting are final and the coins are spent.

04Converting coins and cashing out

There is an important difference between coins you buy and coins you earn or receive:

  • Purchased coins are spend-only. They can be used for gifts and promotion but cannot be converted to wallet balance or withdrawn for cash.
  • Earned or received coins may, where we offer it and you are eligible, be converted to wallet balance and withdrawn. Conversion and withdrawal are available only to eligible users (currently Nigeria-based users), require identity verification (KYC), and are subject to the conversion rate, fees, and limits shown in the app.
  • Users outside Nigeria do not have a cash-out option for coins.
Because purchased coins can’t be cashed out, buying coins should be treated as buying digital content to use in the app — not as storing or sending money.

05Prices, refunds, and app stores

  • Coin prices and bundles are shown before you buy and may change over time.
  • Coins that have been spent, gifted, or promoted are non-refundable.
  • Purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play are subject to Apple’s or Google’s terms, including their refund processes — refund requests for those purchases are handled by Apple or Google.

06Rules

To keep coins fair and lawful, you must not:

  • buy, sell, trade, or transfer coins outside Amril, or for real-world value;
  • use coins, gifting, or conversion to launder money or disguise the source of funds;
  • use fraud, stolen payment methods, or chargebacks to obtain coins;
  • exploit bugs, promotions, or the conversion system.

If we detect abuse, we may suspend coin features, reverse transactions, remove or freeze coins or balances obtained improperly, and where required, report to the authorities. Refunds of any in-app budgets (for example, an unused promotion budget) are returned as non-cashable coins.

07Taxes

You are responsible for any taxes that apply to amounts you withdraw.

08Changes

We may change how coins work, including pricing, rates, limits, and eligibility, and will update the date above. Where changes are material, we’ll aim to give notice in the app.
